Travel Tips

🎓
Student Card Required

Show your valid WLV student ID card to travel free on all campus shuttle routes.

⏰
Arrive 5 mins Early

Buses depart on schedule – aim to be at the stop at least 5 minutes before departure.

🌦️
Weather Delays

Severe weather may cause delays. Check the WLV Twitter/X feed for real-time service alerts.
