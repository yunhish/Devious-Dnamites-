# From the survey, we can come up with a few key takeaways

1. Top Priorities: There should be better ways to check grades and new notifications.
2. Current Problem: 60% of students hear about lecture changes through friends, meaning we need to come up with a better system.
3. Device Strategy: Must work well on mobile (33.3%) and desktop (50%).
4. Authentication: 50% of students wants an email login and 50% do not really care. This is something we will be choosing to prioritise.
5. Open Feedback Themes: Updates, notifications, grades, campus navigation.
